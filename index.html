Welcome to MineSweeper game!

The goal of the game is to reveal all the cells in the field and put flags on all of the mines.

You start the game with:

3 Lives.

3 Safe clicks.

3 Hints (Not allowd on your first click).

The game is divided to three levels:

Beginner: 16 cells to clear (2 mines).

Medium: 64 cells to clear (12 mines).

Expert (only tablets and above): 144 cells to clear (30 mines).

Restrictions:

If you click on a mine - you lose one life.

If you click on mine three time - game over.

Try finishing the game as quick as possible.

If you want to restart the game, you can do so by clicking the Emoji or by selecting a level.

Good Luck!
